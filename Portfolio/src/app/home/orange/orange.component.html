Being a Support Engineer at Orange Services

Since September 15th, I have been working as a Support Engineer at Orange Services in Bucharest — my first full-time position. In this role, I apply ITIL principles, perform log analysis, and contribute to developing monitoring and automation solutions that enhance system reliability and efficiency.

I’m truly grateful to my colleagues, who have been incredibly supportive since my first day, always ready to help with any questions or curiosities I had. I also participated in OpsCon 2025, an internal technical conference held in Bucharest that focused on the company’s future and the implementation of innovative ideas in our work. I was particularly interested in the discussions about RPA integration, as it closely relates to my studies at university.
